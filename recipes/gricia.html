Gricia Pasta

one of the most traditional italian recipes

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Guanciale
  • Pecorino cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Make some water boil in a pot
  2. Cut Guanciale as not too small cubes
  3. Put the Guanciale cubes into a pan with no oil or butter and let it cook on low heat
  4. Salt the water in the pot and throw the pasta in it
  5. When pasta is "al dente", transfer it into the pan with Guanciale and a tablespoon of pasta water and let it finish cooking on high heat
  6. When pasta is completely cooked, turn off the stove and wait 30/60 seconds
  7. When the temperature is lowered, put the cheese and some black pepper in the pasta and mix everything together
  8. Put pasta in the plate and add some extra Pecorino and black pepper on top
